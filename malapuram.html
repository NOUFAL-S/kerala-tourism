PLACES TO VISIT IN MALAPURAM

Kottakkunnu

Kottakkunnu is a tourist destination in the state of Kerala located at Malappuram. Stationed right at the heart of the city, Kottakkunnu attracts large number of domestic as well as international tourists.

Oorakam Hill

Oorakam Mala is a mount near Malappuram, Kerala, India. It is in Oorakam village, bordering Nediyiruppu and Kannamangalam villages. Visible from the Malappuram - Parappanangadi state highway between Vengara and Panakkad and is around 6 km to the peak of the mount from the highway. There is a Jain temple at the top of the mountain, which is around 2000 years old.

Chamakkayam Riverside Park

A river-side park being envisaged from Chamakkayam to Panakkad along the Malappuram-Vengara Road will have a 600-metre walkway, seats for visitors, gardens, and three river-view points. The promenade will be four metres wide. The space left after the four-metre wide walkway will be used for parking.,
